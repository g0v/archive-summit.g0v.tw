The National Museum of Prehistory

The National Museum of Prehistory (NMP) is the first world-class museum of archaeology in Taiwan. Together with the Peinan Archaeology Park, the facilities and exhibition unveil the story of Taiwan’s prehistoric as well as contemporary indigenous cultures.

It is hoped that our efforts in research, collection, exhibition, and education serves as a venue for visitors to gain better understanding of the great diversity of Taiwan’s prehistoric and indigenous cultures, so that we may all come to cherish and respect the land and protect the cultures and environment.

We have been devoted to promote the “All Smart Museum Program” since 2016. By introducing online ticket system, digital interactive system, the APP mobile guiding system, digital reconstitution to archeology relic, and the cultural and creative design system, we try to implement cloud based application to this museum. Open data and creative commons will be the next step we embrace. We aim to enhance the quality of services and bring a more friendly and interesting museum to the visitors.

Taiwan Association of Disaster Prevention Industry

The purpose of the Taiwan Association of Disaster Prevention Industry (TADPI) is to engage and integrate the domestic and International disaster prevention related industry chain actively. TADPI attempted to expand the demand and create the new markets worldwide, and promote the disaster prevention technologies that belong to our members. Officially founded on 31 March 2013, the TADPI coordinated organizations and experts in different field and technology related to disaster prevention, such as hazard modeling and simulation, engineering practices, risk management and insurance, geospatial information, monitoring and communications, security and safety, and firefighting. Through the promotion of the relevant businesses, public awareness and adaptability can be strengthened when facing a catastrophe. Furthermore, advocating the strategy of climate change adaptation, formulation of a Business Continuity Plan (BCP) for the cooperation, and to facilitate and building resilient and sustainable societies worldwide. That should be the ultimate goal of TADPI.
